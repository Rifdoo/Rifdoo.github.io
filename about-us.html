About Me

Rifda

I am Rifda Lutfia, born on 28 February 2008 — 17 years, carrying a heart shaped by sky, earth, and quiet mornings. I feel alive when I’m walking dusty trails or riding a bike across hills at dusk, when the wind whispers through trees and I catch the last golden sliver of sunlight before night. Often you’ll find me with a sketchbook in my hands, quietly tracing the curve of leaves or the swirl of clouds above. And sometimes I’m moving — running, kicking a ball, pushing myself until my lungs burn and my heart remembers what being free feels like. I try to be someone who brings calm — someone who listens, notices the small things: a friend’s quiet mood, a craving for simplicity, a need for laughter or just a little kindness. I offer what I have: a listening ear, a shared joke, a song sent because I thought it might brighten someone’s day. I feel connected to the color blue — the gentle ocean, the wide sky after rain — calm but infinite. Blue feels like me: deep, open, hopeful, ready for what comes.
